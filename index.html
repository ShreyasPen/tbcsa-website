Our Mission

At Tampa Bay CS Club (TBCSC), our mission is to cultivate a thriving computer science community throughout the Tampa Bay area. We believe that technology has the power to change lives, and we aim to equip students with the skills to succeed in a tech-driven future. Through hands-on programming workshops, mentorship, and collaboration, we help students develop the technical skills they need to excel in both academics and their future careers.

What We Do

USACO Training

We offer dedicated training for the USA Computing Olympiad (USACO), where students learn key problem-solving techniques and algorithms. Our program supports students from beginner to advanced levels, helping them improve their coding skills and prepare for competitive challenges.

Tech Webinars

Our tech webinars cover a wide range of topics, including artificial intelligence (AI), cryptography, and basic web development. These sessions provide valuable insights and practical knowledge of cutting-edge technologies.

Mentorship

Club members receive guidance from experienced peers who provide personalized support, study resources, and technical expertise to help them reach their full potential in the field of computer science.
