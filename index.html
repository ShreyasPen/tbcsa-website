Welcome to Tampa Bay CS Club

Empowering the next generation of computer scientists in the Tampa Bay area.

Our Mission

At the Tampa Bay CS Club (TBCSC), we are dedicated to teaching and mentoring underprivileged students in the Tampa Bay area in computer science. We believe that access to education in fields like artificial intelligence, cryptography, and web development should not be determined by socioeconomic status. Our goal is to make computer science more accessible and to equip the next generation with the skills they need to succeed in an increasingly digital world.

What We Do

  • USACO Training

    We provide comprehensive training for students to excel in the USA Computing Olympiad (USACO), helping them develop critical problem-solving and programming skills.

  • Tech Webinars

    We host webinars on cutting-edge tech topics like AI, cryptography, and basic web development, providing students with insights into current industry trends and skills.
