Our Mission

At Tampa Bay CS Club, we provide opportunities for underprivileged students in the Tampa Bay area to explore the world of computer science. Through workshops, mentorship, and hands-on experiences, we equip students with the skills necessary to excel in a tech-driven world.

What We Do

USACO Training

We offer specialized training for the USA Computing Olympiad (USACO) to help students develop problem-solving skills and improve their coding ability.

Tech Webinars

Our webinars feature industry experts discussing AI, cryptography, web development, and more, offering students a deeper understanding of cutting-edge technologies.
