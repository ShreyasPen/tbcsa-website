Our Events

AI and Cryptography Workshop

This event was a two-day workshop where we explored the basics of artificial intelligence and cryptography. Students participated in hands-on coding challenges and learned from expert speakers in the field.

USACO Training Bootcamp

Our USACO Bootcamp was a 3-week intensive training program designed to prepare students for the USA Computing Olympiad. We covered a wide range of topics, from dynamic programming to greedy algorithms, with expert mentors guiding the students.

Web Development Fundamentals

In this workshop, we introduced students to the basics of web development. Topics covered included HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and how to build simple interactive websites. Students left with their own personal projects!
